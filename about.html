The maker

Made by hand,
with care.

Hi, I'm Tetiana — the person behind every stitch at TetiGurumi.

I make handmade crochet toys, decorations and gifts: amigurumi figures, seasonal ornaments, baskets, bags, and pieces made just for you on request. I started crocheting as something personal — a way to make things that feel considered and warm in a world full of mass-produced objects. Over time, people started asking me to make things for them, and TetiGurumi grew from there.

Each piece I make gets the same attention I'd give to something for my own home. The materials are chosen carefully, the finishing takes time, and nothing leaves my hands until it's right.

Materials I use

Cotton yarn

Soft, breathable, skin-friendly. Used for most toys and baby items.

Safety eyes

Rated for toy use, 6–12mm depending on piece size.
