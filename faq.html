Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering.

Jump to: About the work · Orders & Timing · Shipping · Safety & Suitability · Personalization & Gifts · Materials & Care

About the work

Are items handmade by you? expand_more
Yes — every single piece is made by me, Tetiana. I crochet each item by hand, from the first stitch to the final detail. Nothing is mass-produced. When you order from TetiGurumi, you're getting something made with real time and care.
Can I order a custom piece? expand_more
Absolutely. Custom orders are one of my favourite things to make. You can choose the character, colors, size, and even add name embroidery or a personalized gift tag. Use the custom order form to send me your idea — I'll reply with a quote and timeline within 2 business days.

Orders & Timing

How long does production take? expand_more
For ready-made items, I ship within 1–3 business days. For made-to-order pieces, production typically takes 7 to 21 days depending on complexity and my current queue. I'll always confirm the timeline before you commit to an order. If you have a specific deadline (birthday, baby shower, Christmas), let me know and I'll do my best to accommodate it.
Can I change my mind after sending a request? expand_more
Yes — during the approval step (step 3 of our process), we can adjust everything before production starts. Once production begins, changes may not be possible, but I'll always communicate openly if something comes up.

Shipping

Do you ship internationally? expand_more
Yes. I ship within Poland and to most EU countries. International shipping outside the EU is available on request — just mention your country in the custom order form or contact message and I'll check availability and costs.

Safety & Suitability

Are the toys safe for children? expand_more
I use safety eyes rated for toys and hypoallergenic polyester stuffing. However, items with small attached parts (eyes, buttons, decorative elements) are not suitable for children under 3 years old. If you're ordering for a very young child, let me know — I can often adapt the design to remove small parts entirely.
Can colors be changed? expand_more
Yes, for made-to-order pieces. Tell me your preferred palette in the custom order form or in your order notes. I work with a wide range of cotton and acrylic yarn colors and will do my best to match your vision. For ready-made items already in the shop, colors are as shown in the photos.

Personalization & Gifts

Do you offer gift wrapping? expand_more
Yes. Many of my pieces are already gift-ready — packaged in tissue paper with a ribbon and a small tag. For custom orders, you can request a more detailed gift presentation, including a handwritten card (just ask in your order notes).
Can you recreate something from a photo? expand_more
Often yes — especially if it's a character, animal, or style I already work with. Send me a reference photo through the custom order form and I'll let you know if it's doable, how closely I can match it, and what the price would be. I credit original pattern designers when I work from an existing pattern.

Materials & Care

How should I clean my item? expand_more

Toys and decor: Hand wash in cold or lukewarm water (max 30°C) with a gentle detergent. Reshape while damp and air dry flat — do not tumble dry.

Bags: Spot clean or hand wash gently. Avoid soaking if the bag has internal structure.

Avoid machine washing, wringing, or ironing. Most items hold their shape beautifully with gentle care.
